3D Animated Avatar

My 3D Animated Avatar is of a fictional plant that was bioenginered by humans. This plant will only ever bloom in a Northern facing direction. This plant was created to help humans navigate after years of global warming destroying our planet. We knew after a certain point we could not fix it, so we had to give the next human race the chance of survival.









3D Immersive Environment

My 3D Immersive Environment is dualy inspired by both A Game of Thrones and their Hall of Faces and Planet of the Apes. I wanted to create a immersive environment that gave credit to both man and ape for their dominance as creatures, I also wanted to portray a type of intensity between the two through different affects.
